Our Story
GetFunds.ng is a Nigerian platform that offers a participatory and responsive crowdfunding system with it's sole aim towards ensuring individuals and entrepreneurs get the best services to raise funds that can finance their personal, or business needs.

GetFunds creates an amazing platform for donations from philanthropists towards a particular cause thereby assisting others achieve their said goal which comes through charity, and humanitarian causes to save lives.
