Our Story
GETFUNDS.NG is a broad Nigerian profit platform which helps; entrepreneurs and potential inestors plan and make decisions on how to meet the needs of a business idea, charity donations and crowdfunding are made through this platform for important personal needs such as expensive medical bills, school fees aid, instead of waiting to get a large sum of money, an individual can crowdfund and get little donations from a large crowd to meet up that need, GETFUNDS gives you that leverage to meet donors who are willing to give to that course. peer to peer lending popularly known as p2p GETFUNDS offer the services of being able to match borrowers with lenders, individuals will be able to obtain loans directly from lenders without involving the services of a middleman, this platform will take charge of all transaction and help the lenders redeem your loans without any hassles. Profit/revenue sharing can also be done here; proceeds from the sales of goods/services funded on the platform would be shared simultaneously according to the laid out agreements.
